SAMPLE LEGAL CONTRACT

AGREEMENT OF SALE

This Agreement of Sale is entered into on this 15th day of November 2025

BETWEEN:

SELLER: ABC Properties (Pty) Ltd
Registration Number: 2020/123456/07
Address: 123 Main Street, Johannesburg, 2000

AND

PURCHASER: John Smith
ID Number: 8501015800089
Address: 45 Oak Avenue, Sandton, 2031

1. SALE OF PROPERTY

The Seller hereby sells to the Purchaser, who hereby purchases from the Seller, the immovable property known as:

Erf 123 Sandton Extension 5
Measuring: 500 square meters
Street Address: 10 Pine Road, Sandton, 2031

Hereafter referred to as "the Property"

2. PURCHASE PRICE

The purchase price for the Property is agreed upon as:

R 5,000,000.00 (Five Million Rand)

Payable as follows:
- Deposit: R 500,000.00 (on signing)
- Balance: R 4,500,000.00 (on registration of transfer)

3. TRANSFER

3.1 The Seller undertakes to give transfer of the Property to the Purchaser free from all encumbrances except as otherwise stated in this agreement.

3.2 Transfer shall take place as soon as possible but not later than 90 (ninety) days from date of this agreement.

3.3 All transfer costs, including transfer duty, conveyancers fees, and registration fees, shall be for the account of the Purchaser.

4. OCCUPATION

The Purchaser shall be entitled to occupation of the Property on the date of registration of transfer.

5. SELLER'S WARRANTIES

The Seller warrants that:
5.1 The Property is free from any structural defects
5.2 All municipal accounts are paid up to date
5.3 The Property complies with all building regulations

6. CONDITIONS PRECEDENT

This sale is subject to the following suspensive conditions:
6.1 The Purchaser obtaining a mortgage bond of R 4,000,000.00 within 30 days
6.2 The Property passing a satisfactory home inspection

7. DEFAULT

In the event of default by either party, the non-defaulting party shall be entitled to cancel this agreement and claim damages.

8. GENERAL

8.1 This agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties
8.2 No variation of this agreement shall be valid unless in writing
8.3 This agreement shall be governed by the laws of South Africa

SIGNED at Johannesburg on this 15 November 2025

___________________
SELLER

___________________
PURCHASER

WITNESSES:
1. _________________
2. _________________
