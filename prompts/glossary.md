# Glossary

## RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
A technique that combines information retrieval with language model generation to produce more accurate, contextually relevant responses based on specific documents or knowledge bases.

## ELI (Explain Like I'm)
A communication approach to simplify complex topics for specific audience levels (e.g., ELI5, ELI15, ELI25).

## Vector Search
A method of searching that converts text into numerical vectors and finds similar items based on mathematical proximity in high-dimensional space.

## Context Injection
The process of inserting relevant document snippets into a language model's prompt to provide specific information for response generation.

## Confidence Score
A percentage-based indicator representing how reliable the AI's response is, based on source document similarity and relevance.

## Source Citations
References to specific legal documents, cases, or statutes that support the AI's response, formatted as [Source 1], [Source 2], etc.

## Legal Entities
Identifiable elements in legal text such as case names, statutes, legal terms, and jurisdictional references.

## Query Expansion
Technique to enhance search queries by adding synonyms, related terms, and alternative formulations to improve retrieval results.

## Student Mode
A feature that simplifies complex legal language for different comprehension levels while maintaining accuracy.
